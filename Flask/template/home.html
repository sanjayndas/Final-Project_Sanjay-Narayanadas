Airline Passenger Satisfaction



Passenger Satisfaction is a cruicial and important factor that affects any Airline company's business. If the passengers are satisfied then it means they will again come back for the same airline, which results in more business.


Here we have taken data from a US Airlines, and studied, explored, trained and built a model to predict the passenger satisfaction using the Random Forest Classifier ML Model.

The different factors that affect passenger satisfaction are the services provided like Inflight wifi service, inflight entertainment, food, cleanliness, etc., and also factors like Delay.
